SHORT STORY BEHIND
Lawtantra



Sometimes when an incident happens with you and then you decide that this incident should not happen to someone else in the coming time, that's why you think of doing something Which will help others to avoid such incident.

Thinking about this, we thought about creating the Lawtantra. It facilitates young heroes with equipment of knowledge, exposure, analytical skills, confidence, and personality building which helps them to have an advantage at a very young age. This branch specifically works for the development of higher school students selected from numerous government schools in Delhi. A selection process is done followed by grooming these students in their respective talents of writing, research, art, etc. by skillful and established people in these areas in summer and winter vacations.

Lawtantra molds the unrecognized talents into the weapon for victory by: Creating a platform for future. Developing skills such as writing, analytical, speaking, and decision-making. Providing an opportunity to operate their journey through counselling and analyzing. Establishing a connection with societal issues and identifying duty towards society.
